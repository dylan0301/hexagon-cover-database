# Open, Closed, and General Position

Status: Definition

The final theorem uses open unit triangles. Maximality and local algebra are usually cleaner for closed unit triangles in the expanded model \(H_L\), \(L>1\).

General-position convention:

- Positive-length overlap counts.
- Point-only contact does not count as an edge overlap.
- Tangencies and vertex coincidences are degenerate.
- Degenerate cases are handled by perturbation or by the open/closed equivalence.

For CE classification, \(T_C\cap e_{i,i+1}\) contributes only if it contains a relatively open interval in \(e_{i,i+1}\).
