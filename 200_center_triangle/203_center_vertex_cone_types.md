# Center Vertex-Cone Types

Status: Definition

Let

\[
C_i=\left\{re^{i\theta}:r\ge0,\ \frac{i\pi}{3}\le \theta\le \frac{(i+1)\pi}{3}\right\}.
\]

The three vertices of \(T_C\) determine a set of occupied cones, modulo \(D_6\).

## Type 1

\[
\{C_0,C_1,C_3\}
\]

up to rotation and reflection.

## Type 2

\[
\{C_0,C_2,C_4\}
\]

up to rotation and reflection.
