# Center Type 1 Parameterization

Status: Definition / algebraic reduction

Use degree-indexed complementary distances from the center:

$$
d_0,d_{60},d_{120},d_{180},d_{240},d_{300}.
$$

The geometric domain is

$$
0\le d_\theta\le1
\qquad
(\theta\in\{0,60,120,180,240,300\}).
$$

The point

$$
\left(\frac12,\dots,\frac12\right)
$$

means that the center triangle includes all six midpoint targets.

## Initial variety

$$
d_{180}d_{240}-d_{120}d_{300}=0,
$$

$$
d_0d_{120}-d_0d_{180}-d_{120}d_{300}=0,
$$

$$
(d_0+d_{240})d_{300}-d_0d_{240}=0.
$$

## Rational parameterization

Set

$$
d_0=u,\qquad d_{60}=s,\qquad d_{120}=v,\qquad d_{240}=w.
$$

Then

$$
d_{180}=\frac{uv}{u+w},
\qquad
 d_{300}=\frac{uw}{u+w},
$$

with

$$
u+w\ne0.
$$

## Secondary constraint

The Type 1 subvariety is defined by

$$
\Delta(u,s,v,w)=0,
$$

where

$$
\Delta(u,s,v,w)=(s(u+w)+u(v+w))^2-(u^2+uw+w^2).
$$
