# Center Type 1 / Type 2 Dictionary

Status: Reference

Both Type 1 and Type 2 use degree-indexed complementary distances

$$
d_0,d_{60},d_{120},d_{180},d_{240},d_{300}.
$$

Type 1 compares to

$$
\left(\frac12,\dots,\frac12\right),
$$

which means all six midpoint targets are included.

Type 2 compares to

$$
\left(\frac13,\dots,\frac13\right),
$$

which means all six distance-$1/3$ radial targets are included.

Do not merge these two normalizations without explaining the geometric meaning.
