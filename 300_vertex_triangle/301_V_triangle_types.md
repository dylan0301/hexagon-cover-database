# V-Triangle Types

Status: Definition

For a \(V_i\)-triangle, define

\[
o=\#\{\text{vertices of }T_i\text{ outside }H\},
\]

and

\[
n=\#\{\text{adjacent rays }r_{i-1},r_{i+1}\text{ having positive-length intersection with }T_i\}.
\]

The names are:

\[
\mathrm{Vd0}: (o,n)=(1,0)\text{ or }(2,0),
\]

\[
\mathrm{Vd1}: (o,n)=(1,1),
\]

\[
\mathrm{Vd2}: (o,n)=(1,2),
\]

\[
\mathrm{T3\text{-}like}: (o,n)=(2,1).
\]

The case \((o,n)=(2,2)\) is expected to be unattainable.
