# Gamma-to-$c$ Bounds

Status: Definition

The center triangle gives inner gamma values

$$
\gamma_i=\operatorname{dist}(O,\partial T_C\cap[O,V_i]).
$$

The $V_i$-triangle radial coordinate $c_i$ is measured from $V_i$ toward $O$. Therefore the available radial length is

$$
1-\gamma_i.
$$

The local parameter satisfies

$$
c_i\le1-\gamma_i.
$$
