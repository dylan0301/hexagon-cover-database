# Lower Bound Graph Idea

Status: Empirical / strategy

Observation: in center-circle or center-triangle cases, the $g$-composition chain seems not to descend below a positive lower bound.

Possible plan:

1. Prove a lower bound in the circle model.
2. Estimate the loss when passing from circle to triangle.
3. Convert this into a necessary condition for skeleton coverage.

This is promising but not a proof.
