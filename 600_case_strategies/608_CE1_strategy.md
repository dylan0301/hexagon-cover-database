# CE1 Strategy

Status: Strategy / target-lemma dependent

Assume

$$
T_C\text{ is CE1}.
$$

Target lemma:

$$
T_C\text{ covers exactly one midpoint }M_i.
$$

Normalize that midpoint to $M_0$. Then the remaining five midpoint holes must be covered by vertex triangles.

Use:

$$
M_i\in T_i\implies a_i+b_i<1,
$$

plus adjacent-midpoint restrictions for $T_{i\pm1}$ and T3-like restrictions when applicable.
