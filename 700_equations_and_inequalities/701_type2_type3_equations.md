# Type 2 and Type 3 Equations

Status: Reference / lemma targets

Type 2 equation:

$$
\gamma^2-\alpha\gamma+\alpha^2=(\beta\gamma+\alpha^2)^2.
$$

Type 3 equation:

$$
\gamma^2-\alpha\gamma+\alpha^2=((\alpha+\beta)\gamma)^2.
$$

Equivalent Type 3 notation:

$$
c^2-ac+a^2=((a+b)c)^2.
$$

Restrictions:

$$
\alpha+\gamma>1,
\qquad
\beta+\gamma>1.
$$

Target claim:

$$
\alpha+\beta>\frac{\sqrt3}{2}.
$$
