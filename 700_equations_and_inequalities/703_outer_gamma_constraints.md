# Outer Gamma Constraints

Status: Reference / failed-strategy context

Outer gamma is produced by vertex triangles under edge split data

$$
b_i+a_{i+1}=1.
$$

The cyclic feasible set is

$$
S=\{(a_1,b_1,\dots,a_6,b_6):b_i+a_{i+1}=1,
\ a_i^2+a_ib_i+b_i^2\le1\}.
$$

Outer gamma tuples are

$$
(\gamma(a_1,b_1),\dots,\gamma(a_6,b_6)).
$$

This was used in the failed inner/outer gamma separation strategy.
