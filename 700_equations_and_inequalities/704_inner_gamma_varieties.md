# Inner Gamma Varieties

Status: Reference

Inner gamma tuples come from center triangles containing \(O\). They can be sampled or parameterized by:

1. kinematic measure on center-containing triangles;
2. triangles with one vertex on one of the six rays;
3. triangles with two vertices on the union of the six rays;
4. Type 1 and Type 2 algebraic varieties.

Sampling triangles and sampling induced gamma tuples are different measures.
