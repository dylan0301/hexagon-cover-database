# Vertex $c$-Coordinate Sampling

Status: Experiment plan

Sample cyclic edge splits satisfying

$$
b_i+a_{i+1}=1,
$$

and

$$
a_i^2+a_ib_i+b_i^2\le1.
$$

Sequential sampling is convenient but not automatically uniform. Hit-and-run or exact polytope/semialgebraic sampling may be needed for unbiased experiments.
