# Notation Dictionary

Status: Reference

- $O$: center of the hexagon. Some older notes use $C$.
- $T_C$: center triangle, not to be confused with the center point.
- CE0/CE1/CE2: preferred names.
- Ce0/Ce1/Ce2: historical aliases.
- $d_0,d_{60},\dots,d_{300}$: degree-indexed complementary distances from the center in center-triangle parameterizations.
- $\gamma_i$: inner gamma unless explicitly marked outer gamma.
- $1-\gamma_i$: available complementary radial distance.
- $a,b,c$: local V-triangle edge-edge-radial coordinates.
- $F$: coverage-coordinate zero-diagonal map.
- $g_c$: defect-coordinate admissible-set map.
