\subsection{A nonadaptive completion of the mixed overlaps}
\label{sec:strategy4-nonbranching-completion}

The outward-rounded calculation recorded in
Theorem~\ref{thm:cert-mixed-overlap} is a valid independent audit, but it is
not a logical dependency of Theorem~\ref{thm:witness-enclosure}.  We record
here the replacement route used by the proof.

On each radial cell, the rational upper envelopes
\[
 \bar c_L=s-\frac{\chi}{4s^3-2s+m},
 \qquad
 \bar c_T=s-\frac{\chi}{1-m}
\]
satisfy $c_*\le\bar c<1$.  Hence the centered disk of radius
$\bar\eta=h(1-\bar c)$ is contained in the disk of radius
$\eta=h(1-c_*)$ already forced into the center triangle.  Using these smaller
disks, the two mixed cap residuals factor as
\[
 \bar P_A=h^2\lVert A\rVert^2
 \left(\Delta_0^2-\lVert tA-eR^{-1}C\rVert^2\right),
\]
\[
 \bar P_C=h^2\lVert C\rVert^2
 \left(\Delta_0^2-\lVert tR^{-1}C-eA\rVert^2\right),
 \qquad e=1-\bar c,\quad t=2\bar c-1.
\]
Exact elimination with $D^2=z^2+6U+3U^2$ reduces each bracket to a positive
factor times
\[
 A_{B,X}(U,z)+D B_{B,X}(U,z),
 \qquad B\in\{L,T\},\quad X\in\{A,C\}.
\]
Appendix~\ref{sec:exact-mixed-overlap} proves all eight polynomial signs by
three fixed analytic charts and twenty global tensor-Bernstein identities.
There is no interval arithmetic, subdivision, pruning, adaptive depth, or
branch-and-bound.  The two mixed caps therefore overlap for the smaller disks,
and hence for the original disks.  Together with the analytic adjacent
overlaps already proved above, this gives the full cap chain and an independent
nonadaptive proof of Theorem~\ref{thm:witness-enclosure}.  Consequently the
center-independent obstruction and the exhaustive assembly below do not
depend on the outward-rounded audit.
