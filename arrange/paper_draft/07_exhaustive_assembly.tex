\section{Exhaustive assembly}
\label{sec:assembly}

We now follow Table~\ref{tab:routing}.  Its center split is exhaustive by
Proposition~\ref{prop:ce-classification}; its vertex refinements are
exhaustive by Proposition~\ref{prop:vertex-classification}; and the gap
counts are exhaustive by Lemma~\ref{lem:gap-exhaustion}.  Every use of
$N_+$ refers to the actual reaches $(A_i,B_i)$, while
Proposition~\ref{prop:strict-handoffs} supplies the smaller demands used by
Strategies~3 and~4.

\begin{proof}[Proof of Theorem~\ref{thm:main}]
Assume that seven open unit equilateral triangles cover $H$ and label their
distinct center and vertex roles as in Lemma~\ref{lem:distinct-roles}.

\emph{CE0 with $N_+=0$ or $N_+\ge2$.}
The first state contradicts the Strategy~1 interface,
Proposition~\ref{prop:length-interface}; the second contradicts the
Strategy~3 interface, Proposition~\ref{prop:area-interface}.

\emph{CE0 with $N_+=1$.}
The exhaustive vertex trichotomy is: all roles Vd0; at least one Vd1/Vd2
role; or no Vd1/Vd2 role and at least one T3-like role.  These are excluded,
respectively, by Propositions~\ref{prop:nine-point-interface},
\ref{prop:length-interface}, and \ref{prop:area-interface}.

\emph{CE1/CE2 with $N_+=0$ or $N_+\ge2$.}
For $N_+=0$, the all-Vd0 and T3-like-without-Vd1/Vd2 refinements contradict
Proposition~\ref{prop:demand-interface}, while the Vd1/Vd2 refinement
contradicts Proposition~\ref{prop:length-interface}.  For $N_+\ge2$, the
conditional skeleton part of Proposition~\ref{prop:length-interface}
applies.

\emph{CE1/CE2 with $N_+=1$ and all roles Vd0.}
Lemma~\ref{lem:gap-exhaustion} gives zero or one gap for CE1 and zero, one,
or two gaps for CE2.  Zero gaps contradict
Proposition~\ref{prop:nine-point-interface}; every positive-gap state
contradicts Proposition~\ref{prop:demand-interface}.

\emph{CE1/CE2 with $N_+=1$ and mixed vertex types.}
A Vd1/Vd2 role in CE1, or at least two such roles in CE2, is excluded by
Proposition~\ref{prop:length-interface}.  The remaining CE2 state has exactly
one Vd1/Vd2 role: Strategy~1 closes the neighboring-midpoint and
additional-positive-support subcases, and Strategy~2 closes the complementary
exact placements.  If no Vd1/Vd2 role occurs, then the non-Vd0 roles are
precisely T3-like.  Exactly one is excluded by
Proposition~\ref{prop:demand-interface}, and at least two by
Proposition~\ref{prop:length-interface}.

Every row of Table~\ref{tab:routing} is impossible.  Hence the assumed open
cover does not exist.
\end{proof}

Corollary~\ref{cor:expanded-closed} follows from
Proposition~\ref{prop:open-closed-scaled}.
