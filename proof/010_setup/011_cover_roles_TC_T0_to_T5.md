# Cover Roles

Status: Definition

Under a hypothetical seven-triangle cover, choose role triangles

$$
T_C,T_0,T_1,\dots,T_5.
$$

The role $T_C$ is a unit equilateral triangle containing the center $O$.
For each $i\in\mathbb Z/6\mathbb Z$, the role $T_i$ is a unit equilateral
triangle containing the vertex $V_i$.

This is bookkeeping. If more than one triangle contains a special point, choose
one representative for that role.

The underlying geometry objects are defined in
[`100_foundations/101_geometry_objects.md`](../100_foundations/101_geometry_objects.md).

The center-triangle and vertex-triangle local conventions are recorded in:
