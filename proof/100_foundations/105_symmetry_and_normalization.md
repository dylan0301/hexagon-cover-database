# Symmetry and Normalization

Status: Definition

Let $D_6$ be the dihedral symmetry group of the regular hexagon.

Allowed normalizations:

- Rotate/refelect a $V_i$-triangle to a normalized $V_0$-triangle.
- Normalize CE1 to an overlap with a chosen edge, usually $e_{0,1}$.
- Normalize CE2 to two adjacent edge intervals, usually $e_{5,0}$ and $e_{0,1}$, or locally to $I_L\subset e_{3,4}$, $I_R\subset e_{4,5}$.
- Normalize center vertex-cone Type 1 and Type 2 modulo $D_6$.

Normalization must transform all orientation-sensitive labels at the same time.
