# Geometry Objects

Status: Definition

The regular side-$1$ hexagon is

$$
H=\operatorname{conv}\{V_0,\dots,V_5\}.
$$

The radial segment from the center to $V_i$ is

$$
r_i=[O,V_i].
$$
We also call them the "Half Diagonals".

The midpoint of $r_i$ is

$$
M_i=\frac12 V_i.
$$

The boundary edge from $V_i$ to $V_{i+1}$ is

$$
e_{i,i+1}=[V_i,V_{i+1}].
$$

The three main diagonals are

$$
[V_0,V_3],\qquad [V_1,V_4],\qquad [V_2,V_5].
$$

For the expanded side-$L$ hexagon $H_L$, replace each $V_i$ by $L V_i$. When local unit-triangle formulas use a rhombus side length, the notation is

$$
L=1+\epsilon.
$$
