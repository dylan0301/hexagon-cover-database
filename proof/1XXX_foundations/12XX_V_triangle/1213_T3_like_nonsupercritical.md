# T3-Like Rows Are Nonsupercritical

Status: Proven

For a $V_i$-triangle of T3-like type,

$$
(o,n)=(2,1),
$$

the perimeter row is nonsupercritical:

$$
\boxed{a_i+b_i\le1.}
$$

Equivalently, a T3-like row is never counted by

$$
N_+=
\left\lvert
\left\lbrace
i:a_i+b_i>1
\right\rbrace
\right\rvert.
$$

A complete proof is given in
[`../../3XXX_CE0/31XX_Nplus1/317X_T3_like_no_Vd1Vd2/3175_direct_T3_like_area_loss.md`](../../3XXX_CE0/31XX_Nplus1/317X_T3_like_no_Vd1Vd2/3175_direct_T3_like_area_loss.md).

That proof classifies the two orientation families of unit equilateral
$V_i$-triangles.  The first family cannot have T3-like type.  In the second
family, the exact adjacent boundary reaches $A,B$ satisfy

$$
A+B\le\sqrt{1-t+t^2}\le1.
$$

Since the required row coordinates satisfy $a_i\le A$ and $b_i\le B$, it
follows that

$$
a_i+b_i\le A+B\le1.
$$
