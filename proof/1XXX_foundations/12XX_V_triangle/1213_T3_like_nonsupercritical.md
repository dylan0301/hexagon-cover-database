# T3-Like Rows Are Nonsupercritical

Status: Practically proven

For a $V_i$-triangle of T3-like type,

$$
(o,n)=(2,1),
$$

the perimeter row is nonsupercritical:

$$
a_i+b_i\le1.
$$

Equivalently, a T3-like row is never counted by

$$
N_+=\left\lvert \left\lbrace\, i : a_i+b_i>1 \,\right\rbrace \right\rvert.
$$

This fact may be used as a working dependency in the proof tree. The complete
written proof is not recorded in this file.
