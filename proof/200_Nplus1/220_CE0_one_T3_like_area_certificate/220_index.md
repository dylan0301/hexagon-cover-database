Status: Reference

- [`650_area_conjecture_T3_like/653_CE0_one_supercritical_T3_certificate.md`](../../650_area_conjecture_T3_like/653_CE0_one_supercritical_T3_certificate.md).

- the Vd0 square-loss bounds;
- the full T3-like tangent-envelope conjecture.

Those local inputs are not proved in the package. Consequently this branch is
not an unconditional proof of the CE0 one-supercritical T3-like case.
