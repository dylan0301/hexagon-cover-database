# T3-like Midpoint Lemma

Status: Practically proven

Target statements:

1. T3-like triangle $T_i$ cannot contain $M_i$.
2. If $T_i$ is T3-like, then after maximality over $S_{1/2}$ one may assume it contains one of $M_{i-1}$ or $M_{i+1}$.

The second statement is a direct corollary of the first statement.
If a T3-like triangle doesn't contain any midpoints, then it is dominated by axis aligned triangles in $S_{1/2}$.
