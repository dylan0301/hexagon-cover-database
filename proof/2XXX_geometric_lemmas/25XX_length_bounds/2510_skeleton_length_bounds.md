# Skeleton Length Bounds

Status: Lemma target

This file records the local skeleton-length bounds suggested by the proof-tree
sketch.

Targets:

- CE1/CE2 center role covers at most $3/2$ of the relevant skeleton length;
- Vd0 with $a+b\le1$ covers at most $2$;
- Vd0 with $a+b>1$ covers at most $3/2$;
- Vd1/Vd2 covers at most $3/2$;
- T3-like covers at most $3/2$.

The sketch also suggests that the CE1/CE2 bound may imply the Vd1/Vd2 and
T3-like bounds by reinterpreting the center as a vertex. This implication is a
target, not a recorded proof.

Strictness and endpoint cases must be handled before these bounds can prove a
branch obstruction.
