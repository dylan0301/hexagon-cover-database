[../../9XXX_failed_ideas/963X_may25_five_point_failure/9633_CE0_five_point_target_and_obligations.md](../../9XXX_failed_ideas/963X_may25_five_point_failure/9633_CE0_five_point_target_and_obligations.md).

recorded as a proven local lemma in
[2607_minimal_enclosing_equilateral_quadrilateral_lemma.md](2607_minimal_enclosing_equilateral_quadrilateral_lemma.md).

before any monotonicity or convexity claim can be used as proof.
