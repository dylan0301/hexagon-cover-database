# CE0 Perimeter-Length Obstruction Target

Status: Lemma target

Assume:

$$
T_C\text{ is CE0}
$$

and

$$
N_+=0.
$$

Since $T_C$ has no positive-length overlap with $\partial H$, the boundary
must be covered by the six vertex roles. The intended obstruction is that

$$
\sum_{i=0}^5(a_i+b_i)\le6
$$

while $\partial H$ has length $6$, leaving no slack in the open-triangle
formulation.

This file is a target, not a completed proof. A completed proof must specify
the boundary-handoff convention and separate equality, endpoint, and open-cover
cases.
