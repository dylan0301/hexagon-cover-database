# Algorithm 2 Diagonal Relaxation

Status: Empirical strategy

Algorithm 2 replaces the failed unimodality route by selecting diagonal points
from three equality-pattern relaxations among the nonsupercritical rows
$V_0,V_1,V_2$.

The branch-specific definition is
[3110_algorithm2_diagonal_relaxation.md](3110_algorithm2_diagonal_relaxation.md).

minimum-enclosing-triangle lower bounds are certified.
