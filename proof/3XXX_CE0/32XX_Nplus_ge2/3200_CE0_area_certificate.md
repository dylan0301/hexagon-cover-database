# CE0 Area Certificate For $N_+\ge2$

Status: Reference

The CE0 six-point area certificate is recorded in
[3208_CE0_conditional_area_certificate.md](3208_CE0_conditional_area_certificate.md).

It proves:

$$
N_+\ge2 \quad\Longrightarrow\quad \sum_{i=0}^5 f(a_i,b_i)<\frac{99}{20}<5
$$

under explicit local square-loss bounds for $f(a,b)$.

The local square-loss bounds themselves remain open proof obligations, recorded
in
[3207_current_status.md](3207_current_status.md).

Therefore this branch is conditionally closed by the cited analytic
certificate, not unconditionally closed.
