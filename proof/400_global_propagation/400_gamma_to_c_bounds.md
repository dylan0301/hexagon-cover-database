# Center Distance-to-$c$ Bounds

Status: Definition

The center triangle gives degree-indexed ray-intersection distances

$$
d_{60i}=\operatorname{dist}(O,\partial T_C\cap[O,V_i]).
$$

The $V_i$-triangle radial coordinate $c_i$ is measured from $V_i$ toward $O$. Therefore the available radial length is

$$
1-d_{60i}.
$$

The local parameter satisfies

$$
c_i\le1-d_{60i}.
$$
