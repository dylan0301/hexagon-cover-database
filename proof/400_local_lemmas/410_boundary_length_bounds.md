Status: Lemma target

branches that try to close by perimeter length.

- a CE0 boundary-handoff lemma for the $N_+=0$ perimeter obstruction;
- boundary-length inefficiency for CE0 or CE1 branches with Vd1/Vd2 roles;
- strictness statements excluding equality-only open-cover endpoints.

The existing V-triangle type definitions are in
[`300_vertex_triangle/301_V_triangle_types.md`](../300_vertex_triangle/301_V_triangle_types.md).
