# Remaining Reductions To The All-Vd0 Package

Status: Strategy

The CE1/CE2 boundary-loss package applies under all-Vd0 assumptions and the
active-interval reductions recorded in its source files. The broader
$N_+=0$ branch still needs reductions showing when every remaining CE1/CE2
configuration can be brought into that package.

Open reductions include:

- justify all-Vd0 or split off non-Vd0 branches;
- state exactly when the CE2 one-interval lemma is assumed;
- separate boundary degeneracies and equality cases $a_i+b_i=1$;
- verify that removing already-covered $C$-intervals preserves the hypotheses
  needed by the boundary-loss package.

The CE2 one-interval lemma is currently recorded as a target in
