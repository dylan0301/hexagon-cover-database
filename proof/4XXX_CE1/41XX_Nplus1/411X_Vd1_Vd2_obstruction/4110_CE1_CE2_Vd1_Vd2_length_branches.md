# CE1/CE2 Vd1/Vd2 Length Branches

Status: Strategy

This folder records the intended CE1/CE2 branches with at least one Vd1/Vd2
vertex role in the $N_+=1$ case.

The sketch proposes boundary or skeleton length obstructions for several
subbranches:

- CE1 with at least one Vd1/Vd2;
- CE2 with at least two Vd1/Vd2;
- CE2 with exactly one Vd1/Vd2, where a discrete $S_{1/2}$ set-cover fallback
  may be needed.

The required length bounds are not recorded as proofs here. They should be
developed through the local targets in
[../../../2XXX_geometric_lemmas/25XX_length_bounds/2500_boundary_length_bounds.md](../../../2XXX_geometric_lemmas/25XX_length_bounds/2500_boundary_length_bounds.md)
and
[../../../2XXX_geometric_lemmas/25XX_length_bounds/2510_skeleton_length_bounds.md](../../../2XXX_geometric_lemmas/25XX_length_bounds/2510_skeleton_length_bounds.md).
