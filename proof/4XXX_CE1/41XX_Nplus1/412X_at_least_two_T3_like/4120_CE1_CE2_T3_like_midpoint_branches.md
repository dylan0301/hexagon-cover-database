# CE1/CE2 T3-like Midpoint Branches

Status: Strategy

This folder records the intended CE1/CE2 branches with T3-like vertex roles
and no Vd1/Vd2 roles in the $N_+=1$ case.

The sketch separates:

- CE1/CE2 with at least two T3-like roles;
- CE1/CE2 with exactly one T3-like role.

The suggested mechanisms are midpoint forcing, diagonal noncoverage, and
length inefficiency of the relevant local types. These are not proved here.
